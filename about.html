Dessert Learning


Here at Dessert Learning, we provide variety menu of dessert from different kind, from well liked dessert that is top pick by others to easy to make simple and delicious dessert. We also provide online classes for beginners whom wish to learn how to make delicious dessert for their own benefits and satisfy.


Our classes is fill with interactive and engaging digital learning experiences that teaches beginners the steps of making dessert, required ingredients and techniques to make dessert using elements like videos, quizzes and interactive games.

Our Objectives

  • Identify Ingredients and Tools.
    Learners will be able to recognize the essential ingredients and kitchen tools used to make Kek Batik.
  • Apply Preparation Techniques.
    Students will demonstrate the correct method of preparing Kek Batik.
  • Enhance Creativity in Decoration.
    Learners will apply creative ideas in decorating and presenting Kek Batik attractively.
  • Evaluate the Final Product.
    Students will check how their Kek Batik looks, tastes, and feels.

Dessert Learning Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients: 20 Oreo biscuits, 3 tbsp melted butter, 250g cream cheese, 4 tbsp sugar, ½ cup whipping cream.

Steps: Crush Oreos + melted butter → press into a pan, Beat cream cheese + sugar, Whip cream, then fold into cream cheese, Pour onto Oreo base, chill 3–4 hours.

Kek Batik

Ingredients: 250g Marie biscuits, 1 can condensed milk, 3 tbsp cocoa powder, 3 tbsp butter, 1 cup water.

Steps: Break biscuits into a bowl, Cook butter, condensed milk, cocoa, and water, Pour the chocolate mixture over the biscuits, Mix until all biscuits are coated, Press into a tray lined with baking paper, Chill, cut and serve.

Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients: 1 cup whipping cream, ½ cup chocolate chips, 1 tbsp sugar.

Steps: Melt chocolate chips in the microwave, Whip cream + sugar until soft peaks form, Add melted chocolate into the cream and mix slowly, Chill for 1 hour.

Caramel Pudding

Ingredients: 2 tbsp sugar (for caramel), 1 cup milk, 1 egg, 2 tbsp sugar, ½ tsp vanilla.

Steps: Melt 2 tbsp sugar in a pan → pour into a cup, Mix milk + egg + sugar + vanilla, Pour mixture into the cup, Steam 15 minutes.

Cinnamon Sugar Bites

Ingredients: Bread (cut into small cubes), Butter, Sugar + cinnamon.

Steps: Melt butter, toss bread cubes in it, Coat with cinnamon sugar, Bake 10 mins or air-fry 6 mins.

Chocolate Cornflakes

Ingredients: 2 cups cornflakes, 1 cup melted chocolate, Sprinkles (optional).

Steps: Pour melted chocolate over cornflakes, Mix gently, Scoop into small cups, refrigerate 20 mins.

Creme Caramel

Ingredients: 1 cup milk, 1 egg, 2 tbsp sugar.

Steps: Melt 2 tbsp sugar until golden and pour into a cup, Mix milk + egg + sugar, Steam 10–12 mins.

Peach Yogurt Popsicles

Ingredients: 1 cup yogurt, 1 tbsp honey, Sliced peaches.

Steps: Mix yogurt + honey, Add peach slices, Freeze in molds.
